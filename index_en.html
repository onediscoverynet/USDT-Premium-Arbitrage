USDT Premium Arbitrage is a guaranteed profit model that combines safety, repeatability, transparency, and profitability.

All USDT deposits and withdrawals are performed directly from the investor's own wallet. There is absolutely no intermediate custody risk, and the investor has 100% control over their assets.

Guaranteed basic 1% market arbitrage profit with a fixed 30% share for the investor. If the profit exceeds 1%, the investor receives 30% of the increased profit, making it a highly favorable structure.

Trading Process & Profit Structure

01

USDT Purchase Phase

USDT is purchased at -2% of the market price using the investor's PHP funds. The purchased USDT is immediately deposited directly into the investor's own wallet.

02

USDT Sale Phase

USDT is sold to a buyer secured by the brokerage team, typically at -1% of the market price. The transaction is made by withdrawing directly from the investor's wallet.

03

Arbitrage Profit Generation

A basic 1% market arbitrage profit is generated from the difference between buying and selling prices. If the profit exceeds 1% depending on the situation, the profit automatically increases.

04

Profit Distribution

The total market arbitrage profit is distributed at a fixed ratio of 30% for the investor and 70% for the brokerage team. As the number of transactions increases, the profit increases as well.
